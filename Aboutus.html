About us

Who are we?

Did you know that our Luu family was the very first family to open a Vietnamese restaurant in Ottawa. Dai Van Luu and Thu Thi Luu (My grandparents) came to Ottawa in April of 1979 along with their children. By 1980 Mr and Mrs Luu decided to open a Vietnamese restaurant on Booth Street called Little Saigon. Then in 2019, my father Khanh Luu opened his own restaurant in ottawa called Crazy Pho you. While working there for 4 years, I gained a lot of experience and knowledge in the restaurant business. That's when I decided to open my own restaurant in 2023 taking inspiration from my father, calling my own, Crazy Pho Pho.

Keeping it Seasonal, Fresh and Local

We refuse to compromise on quality in our restaurant. That's why we source our fresh ingredients from local farmers' markets. No matter what time of year, you can be sure you're eating the best of the season. Mrs Luu Thi Thu is still today making her very special pork springrolls. So when you stop by and have dinner ask for Grandmas springrolls.
